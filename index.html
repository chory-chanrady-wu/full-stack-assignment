    Our Funds

    Pathways to Prosperity

    At Royal Group Funds, we offer a range of investment funds, both public and private funds to provide our investors with efficient solutions to their investment needs. Our funds are managed by a team of experienced professionals, committed to providing you with the best investment opportunities and superior returns.

    Royal Fixed Income Fund

    Royal Fixed Income Fund is an open-ended private fund designed to deliver stable returns to investors.

    The fund primarily focuses on investing in high-quality market instruments such as bonds and fixed deposits of licensed financial institutions operating within the Kingdom of Cambodia. This diversified approach ensures risk mitigation while capitalizing on robust growth potential and economic stability of the Cambodian financial market.

    Royal Distressed Assets Fund

    Royal Distressed Assets Fund is an open-ended private fund aimed at delivering above-market returns by capitalized on market inefficiencies and opportunities in misvalued assets.

    The fund both strategically identifies and invests in distressed assets, by leveraging comprehensive market analysis and expert asset management. It also looks for opportunities to utilize its balance sheet and experience to ensure investors benefit from the potential high returns.

    About Us

    Royal Group Funds (RGF)

    Royal Group Funds (RGF) is a fund management company established in 2020. RGF is licensed by the Securities and Exchange Regulator of Cambodia (SERC) since 2022 to provide investment funds to both local and international investors under the Collective Investment Scheme (CIS). We offer a range of funds, both public and private funds to provide our investors with efficient solutions to their investment needs.

    Affiliated with the Royal Group of Companies, a leading conglomerate in Cambodia, RGF is committed to be an integral part of achieving excellence for all stakeholders. Rather than being a pure fund manager, we position ourselves as your strategic partner, bringing both financial and management expertise to your investment portfolio.

    News & Insights

    Launch of New Collective Investment Schemes (CIS)

    In late 2024, Royal Group Funds launched two new open-ended private funds designed for professional investors: a Fixed Income Fund and a Distressed Assets Fund. These funds aim to provide investors with opportunities to benefit from Cambodia's economic development, with future plans to offer similar funds to the general public.

    Green Bond Issuance

    In June 2024, the publicly listed entity Royal Group Phnom Penh Special Economic Zone (RGPPSEZ) issued Cambodia's inaugural green bond, raising KHR 41 billion (approximately $10 million USD) at the Cambodia Securities Exchange. The bond issuance, supported by the Credit Guarantee and Investment Facility (CGIF) and the Global Green Growth Institute (GGGI), will fund green infrastructure projects, including a new wastewater treatment plant.

    Q1 2025 Economic Snapshot

    Royal Group Funds released its Q1 2025 Cambodia Economic Snapshot, highlighting the national economy's resilience despite global headwinds. The report noted eased GDP growth, a drop in inflation, an increase in foreign direct investment (FDI), and steady capital markets with strong momentum in green bonds.

    Workforce Development Support

    In November 2025, the Royal Group of Companies announced its support for the Ministry of Labour to strengthen workforce development and financial inclusion in Cambodia.

    New Investment Platform

    Royal Group Securities launched "Royal Group Global Market," the country's first government-approved platform allowing Cambodian investors to access international stock exchanges like the NYSE and SEHK.
